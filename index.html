Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Why not just use ChatGPT or Claude directly?

Great question! While ChatGPT and Claude are powerful general-purpose AI tools, ClarityText is specifically optimized for message writing. We're 3x faster (no tab switching, no prompting), purpose-built with pre-configured communication styles, learn your personal writing style over time, and integrate directly into your workflow. Think of it like using Figma for design instead of Photoshop‚Äîboth work, but one is built specifically for your use case.

How is my data handled?

Your privacy is paramount. Free tier messages are processed and immediately deleted. Pro tier users can opt-in to save rewrite history (encrypted). We never train AI models on your data, and everything is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. Your messages are yours alone.

What if I need more than 10 rewrites per day?

Simply upgrade to Pro for $9/month and get unlimited rewrites. The free tier is designed to let you try the tool and see if it fits your workflow. Most active users upgrade within a week.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime with one click. Plus, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

Do you have a browser extension or mobile app?

Not yet, but they're coming soon! Right now, you can use ClarityText through our website on any device. The mobile-optimized version works great in your browser. Browser extensions and native apps are on our roadmap for Q1 2026.

What languages do you support?

Currently, ClarityText works best with English. However, if you're a non-native English speaker writing in English, it's incredibly helpful for making your writing sound more natural and fluent. Support for Spanish, French, German, and other languages is planned for 2026.
